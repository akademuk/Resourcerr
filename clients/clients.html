About us

Resourcer is a free business accelerator that connects companies with the most suitable lead generation partners. You don’t need to waste time and money on search and communication. You just need to share project requirements and we’ll send you the alternatives of the most qualified teams accordingly.

Such cooperation is the most appealing as you cooperate with a company that has experience in working on projects similar to yours. We analyse your needs, select the best players, interview them, send you the proposals, and arrange an intro call with the approved company. This eventually brings higher results and increases ROI along with your revenue.

F/Q

The first selection happens when you share your project requirements. We compare your budget, deadline and goals with the capabilities of lead generation partners. Thereafter, we create a customized form with the questions that help us understand the results the chosen groups can provide you with. The best matches join interviews with our managers who decide on the most appropriate candidates.
So there are three rigorous evaluation rounds that help us determine who the best alternatives for your project are.

We sign an NDA to keep all the information confidential.

Before adding lead generation companies to our list, we check the reviews on different resources and have calls with their former clients to receive feedback.
