About us

Resorcer is a business development partner for lead generation companies that brings them new clients. Once you apply and fit one’s project requirements, you’ll be invited to an interview with our manager to discuss the details and your case studies. If you’re the best match, you’ll be connected to our client to sign the agreement and start working your magic.

Fill out the application form so that we add you to the list of our partners and and start growing your sales pipeline

Why resourcer?

We can become your business
development manager that will provide you
with new clients

Modify presale efforts having no need to spend money and time on the search and communication with the prospects

Increase customer lifetime and satisfaction score being the best match

Get the clients you choose and can provide the best results with

Immediate project launch as the client is ready to cooperate with you

How it works

01

You fill out the application form describing your terms and experience

02

We verify the information and add you to the list of our partners

03

Once you match the general client’s requirements, we send you the form that can help us understand if your company is suitable for the very project

04

We have an interview with you discussing the details

05

If you’re a 100% match, we send your proposal to the client
